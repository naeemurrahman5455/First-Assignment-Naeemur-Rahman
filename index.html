Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about DevConf-2026. If you still need assistance, feel free to contact our team.

?

Who can attend DevConf-2026?

DevConf-2026 welcomes software developers, students, engineers, designers, startup founders, and anyone passionate about technology and innovation.

?

What's included in my conference ticket?

Your ticket includes keynote sessions, technical talks, workshops, networking opportunities, conference materials, refreshments, and a participation certificate.

?

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. All keynote sessions and selected workshops will be recorded and made available to registered attendees after the conference.

?

Can I transfer my ticket to another attendee?

Yes. Ticket transfers are available until seven days before the event. Please contact our support team for assistance.

?

Will food and refreshments be provided?

Complimentary lunch, coffee breaks, and light refreshments are included for all registered attendees throughout the conference.

?

How can I contact the event organizers?

You can reach our team through the Contact section or email us anytime. We're happy to help before and during the event.
